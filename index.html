Story

The Omnic Crisis

The story of the Overwatch universe begins with the creation of highly advanced robots known as omnics, designed to help humanity. At some point, these omnics turned against humans in a global conflict called the Omnic Crisis. Cities were destroyed, and humanity was on the verge of collapse. To fight back, the United Nations formed an elite international task force called Overwatch, bringing together exceptional soldiers, scientists, and heroes to end the war.

Overwatch Golden Age

Overwatch quickly became a symbol of hope. Led by figures like Jack Morrison and Gabriel Reyes, the organization successfully ended the Omnic Crisis and entered a golden age. During this time, Overwatch expanded its influence worldwide, helping maintain peace and responding to crises. Heroes such as Tracer, Reinhardt, and Mercy became widely known and admired, representing the best of humanity.

Talon Conflict

However, Overwatch's success eventually led to internal problems. Accusations of corruption, political pressure, and controversial black-ops missions began to damage its reputation. Tensions grew especially between Morrison and Reyes, whose ideologies clashed more and more over time. At the same time, a dangerous organization known as Talon emerged from the shadows, seeking to destabilize the world and accelerate human evolution through chaos.

The conflict within Overwatch reached its breaking point when a violent confrontation between Morrison and Reyes resulted in a massive explosion at Overwatch's headquarters in Switzerland. Both men were presumed dead, and the incident led to global outrage. As a result, the United Nations officially shut down Overwatch, banning its operations and marking the end of the organization.
